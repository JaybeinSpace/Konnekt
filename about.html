About Konnekt

A visual-first platform for creators who are ready to be seen.

Konnekt helps emerging filmmakers, photographers, editors, and visual studios present their work in a cleaner portfolio format and get discovered through creator search, project pages, and curated recognition.

Explore Creators Submit Your Work

Mission

Help new visual creators get recognition.

Many talented creators are hidden inside scattered social feeds, old portfolio links, and word-of-mouth networks. Konnekt brings creator profiles, projects, and discovery into one focused experience.

Who it helps

Built for emerging creative talent.

Konnekt is for filmmakers, photographers, editors, production studios, artists, and collaborators who want a polished place to show what they make and find the next person to build with.

How discovery works

From browsing to deeper project context.

Explore creators

Search by name, role, location, and tags to find filmmakers, photographers, and editors.

Open profiles

Creator profiles show bios, badges, stats, media galleries, and featured projects.

View projects

Project pages add credits, tools, tags, and media so each piece of work has more context.
